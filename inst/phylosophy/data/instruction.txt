1: Download the OMA files from https://omabrowser.org/oma/current/
Required files:
- OMA groups in Text format (https://omabrowser.org/All/oma-groups.txt.gz)
- Protein sequences in Fasta format (https://omabrowser.org/All/oma-seqs.fa.gz)
- Species information Text format (https://omabrowser.org/All/oma-species.txt)

2: run /scripts/createDic.py
